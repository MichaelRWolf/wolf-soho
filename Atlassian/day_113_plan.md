# Day 113 Plan (Revised – honoring continuity and loss)

Date: January 21, 2026 (Day 113 of Day 0–120 window)

This document is a **bounded execution plan** for closing the loop on the Confluence data loss incident.

It is written with explicit recognition that what was lost is **not just data**:

- it is a working book of ideas,
- reusable class plans and teaching materials,
- accumulated business foundations,
- and a long arc of deliberate thought and care.

For Wendy, these materials have **time continuity**. They are lived-with artifacts, returned to again and again.
This plan aims to protect that reality while still allowing the work to finish.

Outcome framing remains **neutral**: recovery may or may not occur.

---

## Shared Intent (Read First)

- This is **not just a project**.
- This is a response to the loss of something that actively supported Wendy’s work and identity.
- Execution should minimize re‑exposure to harm, not require emotional endurance.
- When in doubt, choose **less action**, not more.
